About Me

My name is Josh, I am a Front-end engineer.
I have a passion for Design, HTML, CSS, & Javascript.


My eye for Design and attention to Detail comes from a background in the Food & Events
industries as a Sushi Chef, Consultant, and Event Planner. These past experiences allow
me to connect with others and easily understand their point of view.


I am always seeking out knowledge and ways to improve myself as well as uplift those
around me. I am driven to constantly level up and grow as an engineer in the ever evolving
landscape of design and building scalable/reusable components.


When I am not busy with a project you can find me outdoors! I love exploring new places and
experiencing new things. I find that getting away when you can allows you to rejuvenate
yourself and re-align your purpose in life.
